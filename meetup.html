Subject

Beer.JS. This meetup setting is at a local bar, all tech enthusiast are welcome. The meeting date and time for Beer.JS Meetup was May 7 from 8pm to 11pm

Frequency

The meetup is a casual meetup, that typically meets on the first Tuesday of every month.

Objective

The meetup is designed for all Bay Area Web Developers to hangout, have some drinks, and discuss all things, mostly tech and software design.

Take Away

Meetups are an excellent resource for those aspiring, to become a Web Developer or to meet and hangout with like minded people. I was able to meet people of all levels of experience and years in the business. There were Developers from freelancing to ten year Google employees having a drink, talking tech. I was able to get contact information from everyone, and I will continue attending lots of meetups.
